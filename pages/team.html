Our Story

Our team members first met during a volunteer activity cleaning up a river in Beijing. While picking up trash and testing water quality, we observed that the river had turned greenâ€”a visible sign of environmental distress. After researching online, we discovered this was a symptom of eutrophication, a serious ecological problem affecting water bodies worldwide.

This observation inspired us, as high school students, to ask: What can we do to help address this critical environmental challenge? After deep discussion and research, we decided to design a smart buoy system to monitor and treat algal blooms in real-time, combining our passion for environmental protection with innovative technology.

Mission

To predict threats in advance and protect water health for our blue planet.

Vision

Intelligent Waters, Clear Future.

Values

Prevent eutrophication without harming the environment, making water clear again.
